Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Three Legged Voyager Private Limited (operating as NextDot / ClinicBook AI, "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when clinics and their patients use the ClinicBook AI platform and its WhatsApp appointment-booking service (the "Service").

In short: We help clinics take appointment bookings, reminders, and rescheduling over WhatsApp. We only process the information needed to provide that service, we never sell personal data, and clinics or patients can request deletion at any time.

1. Who this policy is for

  • Clinics (our customers) — the clinic owner/administrator who signs up for and manages a ClinicBook AI account.
  • Patients — people who message a clinic's WhatsApp number to book, reschedule, or cancel an appointment.

2. Information we collect

From clinics

  • Clinic name, owner name, business email address, and phone number.
  • Account credentials (passwords are stored only as a secure one-way hash).
  • Clinic configuration such as doctors, schedules, and service settings.
  • WhatsApp Business connection details (provided by the clinic through Meta's official connection flow) needed to send and receive messages on the clinic's behalf.

From patients (via WhatsApp)

  • WhatsApp phone number and profile name.
  • Message content sent to the clinic for the purpose of booking (e.g. preferred date/time, doctor, reason for visit).
  • Appointment records created from those messages.

3. How we use information

  • To create, confirm, reschedule, and cancel appointments.
  • To send appointment confirmations, reminders, and related notifications over WhatsApp.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the Service.
  • To provide support to clinics and respond to requests.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not use patient message content for advertising.

4. WhatsApp and Meta

The Service uses the WhatsApp Business Platform provided by Meta. When a clinic connects its WhatsApp Business number, messages between the clinic and its patients are transmitted through Meta's systems and are subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy. We access only the messaging data required to operate the booking service for that clinic.

5. How we share information

  • Between a clinic and its patients — a patient's booking information is visible to the clinic they messaged, and only that clinic.
  • Service providers — we use trusted infrastructure providers (for hosting, databases, email delivery, and the WhatsApp Business Platform) strictly to run the Service.
  • Legal — we may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Each clinic's data is logically isolated; one clinic cannot access another clinic's data.

6. Data retention

We keep account and appointment information for as long as a clinic's account is active and as needed to provide the Service. When an account is closed, or upon a valid deletion request, we delete or anonymise the associated personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it by law.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (HTTPS), encryption of stored WhatsApp access tokens, hashed passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

8. Your rights & how to delete your data

You can request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time.

  • Clinics: email us from your registered email address requesting deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Patients: email us with the WhatsApp number you used, or ask the clinic you messaged to remove your records.

Send data-deletion requests to apps@nextdot.co.in with the subject Data Deletion Request. We will verify and process the request, and confirm once the data has been deleted.

9. Children

The Service is intended for use by clinics and adults booking appointments. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children. Appointment details for minors are provided and managed by a parent, guardian, or the clinic.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version, and material changes will be communicated to clinics through the Service.
